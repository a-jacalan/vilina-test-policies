User Data Deletion - Vilina

How to Delete Your Account and Data

At Vilina, we respect your right to control your personal information. This page explains how you can delete your account and personal data from our systems.

Account Deletion Options

Option 1: Delete Through the App

  1. Open the Vilina app
  2. Go to Profile → Settings → Privacy
  3. Tap on "Delete My Account"
  4. Follow the on-screen instructions to confirm deletion
  5. You will receive a confirmation email once deletion is complete

Option 2: Submit a Deletion Request

If you prefer, you can submit a deletion request by:

  • Emailing privacy@vilina.com with the subject "Data Deletion Request"
  • Include your registered email address and username in the email

Option 3: Data Deletion for Facebook Users

For users who logged in via Facebook:

  1. You can revoke Vilina's access to your Facebook data through your Facebook settings
  2. Additionally, you must still delete your account through our app or by contacting us to ensure complete data removal

What Gets Deleted

When you delete your account, we will:

  • Remove your personal profile information
  • Delete your account credentials and login information
  • Remove your activity history and preferences
  • Delete any content you've created or uploaded
  • Disconnect all third-party login associations

Data Retention After Deletion

Some information may be retained:

  • For legal compliance purposes (typically for 30 days)
  • Anonymous, aggregated data that cannot identify you
  • Information needed for legitimate business purposes, such as fraud prevention

Deletion Timeline

  • Account deletion processing typically begins within 24 hours
  • Complete deletion of all data may take up to 30 days
  • Backup systems may retain your data for up to 90 days

Additional Information

  • Deletion is permanent and cannot be undone
  • If you've made purchases, transactional records may be retained for legal and accounting purposes
  • If you log in again during the deletion process, your deletion request will be canceled
